get_site_rdf(structure: Structure) → Tuple[ndarray, List[Dict]]¶
Args:

structure (Structure): pymatgen structure

Returns:

r, rdfs, r is the radial points, and rdfs are a list of rdf dicts rdfs[0] is the rdfs of the first site. It is a dictionary of {atom_pair: pair_rdf} e.g.,

{“Sr:O”: [0, 0, 0.1, 0.2, ..]}
